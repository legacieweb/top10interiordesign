Frequently Asked Questions

How long does countertop installation take?

Installation time varies by project size. Single countertops take 1-2 days, while multiple installations may take 3-7 days.

Do you provide maintenance services?

Yes, we offer professional countertop cleaning, sealing, and restoration services to keep your countertops looking pristine.

What types of countertop materials do you work with?

We specialize in granite, quartz, marble (Carrara, Calacatta), onyx, travertine, and other premium natural stones.

Are countertops suitable for bathrooms?

Absolutely! Granite and quartz countertops are perfect for bathrooms when properly sealed. They add luxury and are moisture-resistant with the right care.
