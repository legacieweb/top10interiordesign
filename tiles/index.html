Tile Types I Install

From classic ceramic to luxurious marble, I install all types of tiles with precision and professional expertise

Ceramic Tiles

Durable, versatile, and affordable. Perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-traffic areas. Available in countless colors and patterns.

  • Water resistant
  • Easy to clean
  • Cost-effective

Porcelain Tiles

Premium quality with superior durability. Ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications. Resistant to moisture and stains.

  • Extremely durable
  • Low maintenance
  • Frost resistant

Marble Tiles

Luxurious natural stone that adds elegance to any space. Each piece is unique with natural veining patterns.

  • Timeless beauty
  • Increases property value
  • Natural elegance

Mosaic Tiles

Create stunning visual effects with small decorative tiles. Perfect for backsplashes, accent walls, and artistic designs.

  • Artistic designs
  • Versatile patterns
  • Custom options

Designer Tiles

Premium imported tiles with unique textures, patterns, and finishes. Make a bold statement in your space.

  • Exclusive designs
  • Premium quality
  • Luxury finish

Large Format Tiles

Modern oversized tiles that create seamless, spacious looks. Fewer grout lines mean easier cleaning and contemporary aesthetics.

  • Modern appearance
  • Minimal grout lines
  • Spacious feel
